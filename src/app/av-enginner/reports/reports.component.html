What It Is

An SPL (Sound Pressure Level) calculator is a tool that helps you estimate the maximum loudness (in decibels) at a specific listening position. It takes into account factors such as speaker sensitivity, amplifier power, and the distance from the speaker to the listener. By inputting these parameters, you can predict the sound intensity and adjust your audio setup accordingly. Keep in mind that this calculation provides estimates and does not account for room acoustics or off-axis listening positions

The Formula for SPL is:
SPL = 20.log(d1/d2)
Where:

  • SPL = The Sound Pressure Level at a defined distance

  • d1 = distance at which the SPL is given

  • d2 = distance at which you want to know the SPL
